# Data Science & Econometrics Portfolio

This repository contains applied data science, econometric modeling, and forecasting projects completed during my master’s studies in economics with a concentration in business analytics and forecasting.

The work focuses on using data to answer real-world questions through statistical modeling, machine learning, and time-series analysis.

---

## 🔎 Featured Case Studies

These projects represent my strongest applied work and are structured to reflect real-world analytical workflows.

### Oil Price Shock Analysis (Bayesian SVAR)
- Modeled the impact of oil supply and demand shocks on U.S. macroeconomic outcomes  
- Implemented a Bayesian Structural VAR with sign restrictions  
- Extended academic research to evaluate changing economic sensitivity over time  

**Skills:** Time-series modeling, Bayesian econometrics  
**Tools:** R, bsvarSIGNs  

---

### Food Assistance & Food Insecurity (Causal Inference)
- Estimated the impact of food assistance programs on food insecurity  
- Applied treatment effect models using observational data  
- Evaluated robustness across model specifications  

**Skills:** Causal inference, regression analysis  
**Tools:** R, econometrics  

---

### Real Estate Price Prediction (Machine Learning)
- Built predictive models for housing prices using structured data  
- Performed feature engineering, model selection, and evaluation  
- Compared model performance using cross-validation  

**Skills:** Machine learning, model tuning  
**Tools:** Python, scikit-learn, XGBoost
